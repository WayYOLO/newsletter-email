THE HIGHLIGHT

It’s that time of year again! ZTEsoft Annual Summit 2017 will be taking place at a unique castle on French riviera - Château de Crémat on May 18, 2017.

ZTEsoft Annual Summit 2017 brings together executives from major communication service providers that are using ZTEsoft innovative solutions and executive industry experts to network, learn and collaborate on becoming digital service providers. The Summit is a place where thought leaders from around the world are encouraged to share ideas, experiences, and knowledge.

In our more ever-connected world, our life will become truly digital and be full of screens. We keep them all the time from the moment we wake up in the morning until the minute we fall asleep. The digital life will always be on, always open. How do communication service providers transform themselves in an effect to enable customers to enjoy the always-on digital life? ZTEsoft believes the power of CSPs, vendors and industry players can be united together to offer unparalleled experience and competence on the way to digital success.

ZTEsoft Annual Summit incorporates a full morning of sessions for leading industry analysts. It’s a great opportunity for the delegates to learn from and network with analysts across the industry. The Summit offers a more relaxed way of engaging in thought-provoking round-table discussions and gives you access to insightful keynote speeches. ZTEsoft will also share its own vision and thoughts on how to address the industry’s most pressing issues and strategies with POWER for digital revolution.

It definitely will be an enjoyable time, together with you. Join us at the Summit to share and network with executives from major communication service providers and industry experts in a more relaxing way.

ZTEsoft Annual Summit 2017 is invitation only. For more information or questions, please contact us at ztesoftinfo@ztesoft.com or call at +86 139 5177 1387.

We look forward to seeing you in Nice!
